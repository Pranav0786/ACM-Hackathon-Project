RSC

WCE-ACM's Research Symposium On Computing (RSC) unfolds as an annual celebration, inviting the brilliance of both undergraduate and postgraduate students to showcase their innovative projects and ideas in the realm of computing. A platform where creativity and knowledge converge.

HACKATHON

WCE ACM Student Chapter conducted WCE HACKATHON'23, a developing venture in which participants are expected to complete the product development as per the selected problem statements. Here participants will innovate, strategize, code, and beat the odds with some ingenious solutions to the most sought real-life problems.

DSA LAUNCHPAD

ACM DSA Launchpad is an annual event organized by "WCE ACM," where we delve into the world of Data Structures and Algorithms through comprehensive coverage of essential concepts, engaging quizzes, and problem-solving practice.

ACM-TALKS

ACM Talk, an enriching event, beckons our esteemed alumni to share their guidance and provide a clear roadmap for students, fostering a seamless journey of knowledge and inspiration. A convergence where wisdom meets eager minds.
